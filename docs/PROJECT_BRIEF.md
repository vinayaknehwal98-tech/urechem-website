# Urechem Project Brief

## Company Positioning

Urechem is a technical polyurethane and specialty-chemical solutions partner that identifies application problems, researches and develops formulations, supports implementation, validates quality, and supplies products.

The website should position Urechem as a practical, technically capable partner for manufacturers, applicators, procurement teams, and technical decision-makers who need reliable polyurethane and specialty-chemical support.

## Provisional Company Figures Requiring Client Validation

The following figures are provisional and must be validated by the client before public use:

- 40+ projects successfully executed

- 150+ years cumulative experience
- 25+ professionals
- 6+ industries served
- 28+ brands owned
- 50+ projects delivered on time
- 267 checkpoints and 24 checklists
- 24×7×365 support claim

## Eight-Stage Workflow

1. Client problem identification
2. Research and global solution search
3. Raw-material procurement
4. Custom product development
5. Third-party testing
6. Certification and compliance
7. Client sample approval
8. Final product delivery
